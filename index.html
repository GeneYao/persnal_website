About

Bridging the gap between logical structure and emotional resonance. As a software developer by day and a selector by night, my sets are built with the same precision as a well-architected application.

My sonic signature is deeply rooted in 70s analog warmth, Krautrock, Soul, and Dub. Whether testing mixes on Genelec monitors at home or bringing the vibe to a live venue, the focus remains on the purity of the sound and the narrative of the selection.
